BMR => Basal Metabolic Rate

The amount of calories you burn if you were to do nothing all day but be alive.

TDEE => Total Daily Energy Expenditure

The amount of calories you burn when your daily activity is added to your BMR. Read More.

CICO => Calories In Calories Out

The equation for weight manipulation. Fewer calories in than out results in weight loss, vice versa for weight gain

IFTTT => If This Then That

IFTTT, a service that listens for triggers and executes actions.
